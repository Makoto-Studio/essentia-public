Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 1, 2025

1. Introduction

Makoto Studio ("we", "our", or "us") operates essentia, a personal organization application available for iPhone, iPad, and Mac. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use essentia.

By using essentia, you agree to the collection and use of information as described in this policy. If you have questions or concerns, please contact us at developer@makotostudio.dev.

3. What We Do NOT Collect

  • Advertising identifiers (IDFA) — we do not use ad tracking
  • Cross-app or cross-site tracking
  • GPS coordinates or precise location data
  • The content of your tasks or notes in analytics events
  • Contact lists, photos, or media
  • Browsing history
  • Any data sold or shared with advertisers

4. How We Use Your Data

  • Authentication: To create and secure your account
  • Core functionality: To store, organize, and display your tasks and notes
  • Cloud sync (Pro): To sync your data across your devices
  • Push notifications: To deliver reminders you configure
  • Analytics: To understand how features are used and improve the app
  • Crash reporting: To identify and fix bugs
  • Subscription management: To verify and maintain your essentia Pro entitlement

6. Local-First Architecture

essentia is designed local-first. All tasks, notes, collections, and other content are stored on your device in a local database. Cloud sync is an optional feature available exclusively to essentia Pro subscribers.

If you use the free tier, none of your personal content is ever transmitted to our servers. The app is fully functional offline with no degradation in core features.

7. Data Retention and Deletion

  • Sign out: Clears local data from the device. Remote data (Pro) remains in your account until deletion.
  • Delete account: Permanently deletes your account and all associated remote data (tasks, collections, devices). This action is irreversible.
  • Downgrade from Pro: Local data remains intact. Remote sync pauses; previously synced data is preserved on the server but not updated until you resubscribe.

You can delete your account at any time from essentia's Settings. To request data deletion by email, contact developer@makotostudio.dev.

8. Data Security

  • Passwords are hashed using bcrypt and never stored in plaintext
  • All data in transit is protected by HTTPS/TLS
  • Remote data is encrypted at rest by Supabase
  • Local data is stored in the app's sandboxed container, accessible only to essentia

While we implement industry-standard security measures, no system can guarantee absolute security. We encourage you to use a strong, unique password for your account.

9. Children's Privacy

essentia is not directed at children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

10. Your Rights (GDPR)

If you are located in the European Economic Area, you have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Access: Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you
  • Rectification: Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Erasure: Request deletion of your data (right to be forgotten)
  • Portability: Receive your data in a structured, machine-readable format
  • Restriction: Request that we limit processing of your data
  • Objection: Object to our processing of your data

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at developer@makotostudio.dev. You may also lodge a complaint with the Spanish Data Protection Authority (AEPD) at aepd.es.

11. Calendar and Location Access

Calendar (EventKit): If you grant calendar permission, essentia can display and create calendar events on your device. This data stays entirely local and is never transmitted to our servers.

Location (MapKit): If you use location labels for tasks, essentia uses MapKit to search for places based on text you type. Searches are sent to Apple's mapping services per Apple's privacy policy. essentia does not access your GPS coordinates and does not store or transmit any location data to our servers.
