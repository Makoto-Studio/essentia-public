The problem

You don't need another app that adds to the noise.

You already have a calendar. A notes app. A to-do list. Maybe three. And yet, somehow, you start every day staring at a mountain of tasks you didn't finish yesterday — carrying guilt instead of clarity.

The problem isn't that you have too much to do. It's that your tools never ask you to decide: does this actually matter today?

The philosophy

Capture. Plan. Review. Let go.

essentia is built on bullet journaling — the idea that writing things down isn't enough. You need to regularly decide what still deserves your time.

Step 01

Capture

Get it out of your head instantly. Tasks, notes, ideas — without having to decide where they belong. Your inbox holds everything until you're ready.

Step 02

Plan

Schedule intentionally. Set due dates, recurrence, priorities, and location context — only for tasks that truly deserve a place in your day.

Step 03

Review

Every morning, face your unfinished list and make a real decision: Do it today, push it later, or let it go. No guilt, just clarity.

Morning Review

Stop carrying yesterday's unfinished list.

Each morning, essentia surfaces your pending tasks and asks a simple question: does this still matter? Move it to today, push it to later, or release it. Start fresh, not buried.

Features

Everything you need, nothing you don't.

Six focused capabilities that cover the full loop from capture to clarity.

Your day at a glance

Tasks, notes, and calendar events unified in a single daily view. No app-switching, no context loss.

Notes that remember where they belong

Attach notes directly to calendar events. Your meeting prep and follow-ups live right next to the event.

Capture first, organize later

An inbox that accepts anything — task, note, idea — with no required fields. Get it out of your head in under two seconds.

See your free time

essentia calculates your real availability based on calendar events, so you know how much room you actually have today.

Smart Collections

Build dynamic filters with boolean logic. Your "High priority, no due date" collection updates itself automatically.

Works without internet

Local-first architecture means essentia is fully functional offline. Sync happens in the background when you're connected.
