The problem

You don't need another app that adds to the noise.

You already have a calendar. A notes app. A to-do list. Maybe three. And yet, somehow, you start every day staring at a mountain of tasks you didn't finish yesterday — dragging guilt instead of clarity.

The problem isn't that you have too much to do. It's that your tools never ask you to decide: does this actually matter today?

The philosophy

Capture. Plan. Review. Let go.

essentia is built on bullet journaling — the idea that writing things down isn't enough. You need to regularly decide what still deserves your time.

Features

Everything you need, nothing you don't.

Focused capabilities that cover the full loop from capture to clarity.

Your day at a glance

Tasks, notes, and calendar events unified in a single daily view. No app-switching, no context loss.

Triage: your inbox, under control

Everything lands in the inbox first. When you're ready, Triage walks you through item by item: assign a date, a collection, or let it go.

Empty your head in seconds

Capture anything — task, note, idea — with no required fields and no decisions upfront. Under two seconds, out of your head and into essentia.

See your free time

essentia calculates your real availability based on calendar events, so you know how much room you actually have today.

Smart Collections

Build dynamic filters by combining custom rules. Your "Work" and "Important" collections update themselves automatically.

Works without internet

Your data lives on your device. essentia is fully functional offline. Sync happens in the background when you're connected.

Widgets and Share Extension

Capture from your home screen with widgets, or share content from any app straight into your essentia inbox.

Calendar integration

See your events alongside your tasks in the Today view. essentia calculates your real availability so you plan with data, not guesses.
